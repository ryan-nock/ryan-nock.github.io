About

Developer, researcher, builder.

I'm a developer, researcher, and occasional designer with a deep interest in machine learning, systems programming, and building interfaces that feel genuinely delightful to use.

Most of my work lives at the intersection of technical depth and usability — whether that's training models, writing low-level Python, or obsessing over algorithms at 2 a.m.

My research spans attention mechanisms, generative models, and NLP — studying how language models represent and compress information, and where they fail.

When I'm not at a keyboard, I'm probably reading about AI, walking, or looking for good coffee.
